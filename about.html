Hi there!

My name is Olga Sago. I am a recent graduate of Metropolitan State University of Denver, Colorado with a B.S. degree in marketing and a minor in computer science. My areas of focus are in digital marketing, web design and photography.

On a professional level, I would like to help businesses grow through digital marketing, web design and photography, which I view as all being critical parts of helping build up a company's image and online presence in the ever evolving and continusly spreading digital world we live in.
