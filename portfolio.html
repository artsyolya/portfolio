Know When to Book
Android app

The app helps address the issue of knowing how far in advance it is recommended to book flights. Other features include best and worst times to travel to desired destinations, as well as options to explore the destinations on Google Maps and LonelyPlanet.com, and start the booking flights process through Skyscanner.com.

The app was developed as a final project for mobile programming class.
