About Me

My name is Olga Sago. I am a senior student at Metropolitan State University of Denver, Colorado majoring in marketing with a minor in computer science with plans to graduate in the Spring of 2020. My areas of focus are in digital marketing, web design and photography, and I am an aspiring digital content creator.

I was born in Almaty, Kazakhstan and lived there with my mom and brother until the age of 11. In the fall of 2003, we packed our bags and flew to Denver, Colorado to start anew. I ended up living in Colorado for over 15 years and making a home for myself, but I hope to start traveling more upon graduating in May, and seeing as much of the world as I can.

On a professional level, I would like to help businesses grow through digital marketing, web design and photography, which I think can all work together to help build up a company's image and online presence in the ever evolving and continusly spreading digital world we live in.

Feel free to check out my work below and reach out to me if you would like to connect!
