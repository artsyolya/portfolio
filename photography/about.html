Hi there!

My name is Olga Sago, a Colorado based, freelance photographer and a recent graduate of Metropolitan State University of Denver with a B.S. degree in Marketing and minor in Computer Science.

I was born in Almaty, Kazakhstan and lived there with my mom and brother until the age of 11. In the fall of 2003, we packed our bags and flew to Denver, Colorado to start anew. I ended up living in Colorado for over 15 years, but moving continents slowly lead to a growing desire to travel and explore the world and with it a desire to photograph life.

I started taking photos at a young age, but it wasn't until I got my first digital camera for my 16th birthday that I really became interested in the art of photography and it wasnâ€™t until a few years ago that I decided to pursue it professionally. For the past year, I have been fortunate to take photos for MSU Denver, some of which have been published on MSU Denver's online magazine RED, online newsletter Early Bird, social media and used in marketing.

I am still learning everyday and hope to continue growing my portfolio and skills. One of my goals is to travel around the world and see as much of it as possible, while capturing the beauty of the world and those special moments for others because photos help freeze time and help us remember the things we might forget years down the road.

Feel free to check out my portfolio, my published work and reach out to me if you would like to connect!
